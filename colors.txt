# Site Color Palette

--green: #b9ff66
  Used for accents, highlights, and hover states on buttons.

--black: rgb(0, 0, 0)
  Used for primary text, headings, button text, and dark badges.

--dark: #191a23
  Used for dark button backgrounds and dark section elements.

--gray: #f3f3f3
  Used for light gray backgrounds and neutral surfaces.

--white: #ffffff
  Used for page background, white buttons, and light elements.

Other shades in CSS:

#f1f1f1
  Used for the custom scrollbar track background.

#000000
  Used for the custom scrollbar thumb and default black text.

#555555
  Used for the custom scrollbar thumb hover state.
